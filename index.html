My Work

Siemens Zel X and Xcelerator Share

Zel X and Xcelerator Share are cloud softwares in Computer Aided Design Domain that enable Mechanical engineers, designers, and other stakeholders to create, edit, store, and collaborate on CAD Projects.

Entity Extraction for Legal Insurance Documents

I developed the AI pipeline to extract named entities like Advocates' names and Court case numbers from Legal Insurance Documents. The project is successfully integrated in IzDox, the flagship product of BizAmica Software Pvt. Ltd.

Face Embeddings & Similarity Search Algorithms

As an intern at C-DAC, I studied Face Embedding Generation Systems like FaceNet and DR-GAN and compared their performance using the t-SNE technique. I benchmarked the performance of 20 Similarity Search Algorithm Libraries in Python.

Detection of Diabetic Retinopathy

I developed a deep learning model to detect Diabetic Retinopathy in Retinal Images. I developed a simple WebApp interface for the deployed model as well.

MCQ Platform

I was tasked with developing the BackEnd of a WebApp for MCQ based coding competitions. The WebApp was deployed for my college's Annual Technical Fest, Pulzion' 19.

Decentralised Competition Management System

DCMS is a WebApp which aims to verify the participation and prize distribution in a competition seamlessly. DCMS won the 3rd Prize at the Devcon' 21 HackOverflow organised by IEEE NCU Student Branch on DevFolio. DCMS won the Ethereum track prize from Polygon as well.
